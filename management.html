Md. Mostafa Sarwar Tito

Owner, Chief Executive Officer

Mostafa Sarwar Tito is Owner and Chief Executive Officer of A2T CLOTHING. He has served on the company since founded of “A2T CLOTHING” in January 2023. Md. Mostafa Sarwar Tito was named CEO of A2T CLOTHING in January 2023 and led the newly formed Company with an extensive experience of more than 25 years in retail, operations, marketing, merchandising, sales and manufacturing, including extensive executive leadership experience.

  • Prior to being named CEO of A2T clothing, Md. Mostafa Sarwar Tito was Managing Director, for P.K.T FASHION. He joined P.K.T FASHION in December 2016 and server till 2022. In this role, he was responsible for overseeing customers in Canada such as The Warwick International Inc, ARDEN, Thread Collective Inc. TFC Express Inc. etc.

  • MD. Mostafa Sarwar previous leadership roles at NN Fashion as Head of Merchandising & Sourcing. MD. Mostafa Sarwar joined NN Fashion in 2010 and server till 2016. In this role, he was responsible for linking International Customers, Importers and Apparel Manufacturers for their best sourcing, negotiating, quality and compliance, production follow up, reporting, planning & shipment tracking with correct documents. Also expertise on strategic planning, supervising and follow up with a team of Merchandisers, Production managers, Quality controllers and Commercial peoples to ensure a smooth delivery upto stores.

  • Prior to these leadership roles, MD. Mostafa Sarwar served as Managing Director of Global Woven Wear LTD, MD. Mostafa Sarwar joined Global Woven Wear LTD in 2006 and server till 2010. In this role, he was responsible for supervision brands customer and client with the whole management part of the production unit with 550 employees and an approximate annual turnover of USD 2 million with significant profits.
